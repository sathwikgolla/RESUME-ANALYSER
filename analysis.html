ATS-Friendly Resume Tips

  • Use a simple and clean format.
  • Include relevant keywords from the job description.
  • Avoid using images or graphics.
  • Use standard fonts like Arial or Times New Roman.
  • Save your resume as a .PDF or .DOCX format.
  • Use clear section headings (Work Experience, Education, Skills).
